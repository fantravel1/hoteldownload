How We Rank

Scoring Methodology

Our rankings are based on rigorous, data-driven analysis across multiple dimensions.

Price Testing (35%)
Monthly price comparisons across 100+ hotels in 20 cities. We measure average savings, consistency, and hidden fees.
Features & Policies (25%)
Analysis of cancellation flexibility, loyalty rewards, booking ease, and customer protection policies.
Global Coverage (20%)
Inventory depth in 50+ countries, availability in major destinations, and regional specialization.
User Experience (15%)
App performance, booking flow simplicity, customer support responsiveness, and mobile optimization.
User Reviews (5%)
Aggregate ratings from App Store, Google Play, and third-party review platforms, weighted by recency.
Updated Monthly
Rankings are refreshed at the start of each month with new price data, feature updates, and policy changes.
Independence Guarantee
HotelDownload.com does not accept payment for rankings or reviews. All scores are based solely on our testing methodology. We may earn affiliate commissions when you book through our links, but this never influences our rankings.
