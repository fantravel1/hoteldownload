Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common hotel booking questions.

The best hotel booking app depends on your location and travel style. Booking.com offers the widest global inventory with 28M+ listings and free cancellation. Hopper uses AI to predict price drops and tells you the ideal time to book. Google Hotels provides the best meta-search comparison across 50+ booking sites. For loyalty rewards, Marriott Bonvoy and Hilton Honors apps offer exclusive member rates not available elsewhere.

Proven strategies include: (1) Compare prices across 3+ platforms before booking, (2) Use incognito/private browsing to avoid dynamic pricing, (3) Book directly with hotels for price-match guarantees and added perks, (4) Check prices on different devices — mobile-only deals are common, (5) Stack savings with cashback portals and loyalty points, (6) Book on specific days (Tuesday/Wednesday are often cheapest), and (7) Check regional pricing via different country-specific platform versions.

Neither is consistently cheaper. In testing across 50+ markets, Booking.com tends to offer better rates in Europe and Asia, while Expedia often has more competitive bundle deals (hotel + flight) in North America. The best approach is to compare both alongside Google Hotels, which aggregates prices from dozens of sources including direct hotel websites.

Some booking platforms use dynamic pricing that may consider your browsing history and cookie data. While major platforms deny this practice, independent testing has shown price variations of 5–15% based on factors like device type, location, and browsing patterns. Using incognito mode and comparing across devices is a safe, legal way to check for price discrimination.

Use meta-search engines like Google Hotels, Trivago, or Kayak to compare prices from dozens of booking sites simultaneously. Then verify the lowest price directly on the hotel's own website — many hotels offer a best-rate guarantee that matches or beats third-party prices, with added perks like free upgrades or late checkout.
