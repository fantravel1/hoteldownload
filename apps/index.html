Booking App Questions

Expert answers to the most common questions about hotel booking apps.

Booking.com leads with 28M+ listings worldwide, including hotels, apartments, hostels, and vacation rentals across 220+ countries. Airbnb follows with 7M+ unique properties. Among traditional OTAs, the Expedia Group (Expedia + Hotels.com + Vrbo) collectively offers the second-largest hotel inventory.

For the absolute lowest prices, use a meta-search app like Google Hotels, Trivago, or Kayak to compare rates across all platforms simultaneously. For AI-powered deal finding, Hopper predicts price drops with 95% accuracy and offers price freezes. For opaque deals, Priceline's Express Deals can save up to 60% on hotels you cannot identify until after booking.

Many hotel chains (Marriott, Hilton, IHG, Hyatt) offer best-rate guarantees on their own apps, often matching or beating third-party prices while adding perks like free Wi-Fi, room upgrades, and loyalty points. However, OTAs like Booking.com and Expedia frequently offer flash sales and bundle deals that can undercut direct prices by 10-25%. The best strategy is to compare both before booking.

Yes. Most major booking apps offer mobile-exclusive prices that are 10-20% lower than their desktop websites. Booking.com shows "Mobile-only prices" for Genius members, Expedia offers "App-only deals", Hotels.com has "Secret Prices" on the app, and Agoda consistently offers deeper discounts through its mobile app compared to the website.

World of Hyatt is widely regarded as offering the best value per point and the easiest path to elite status with meaningful perks like suite upgrades. Marriott Bonvoy has the largest portfolio (30+ brands, 8,500+ properties) and the most transfer partners. Hilton Honors is the easiest to earn free nights with (points never expire, many credit card options). Hotels.com's simple "stay 10, get 1 free" program is best for travelers who book across different hotel brands.

The best strategy is to: (1) Search on a meta-search app like Google Hotels or Kayak to find the lowest rate across all platforms, (2) Check if the hotel's direct app matches or beats that price, (3) Compare loyalty program value if you are a member, and (4) Look for cashback offers through portals. Different apps frequently have different prices for the same hotel on the same dates.
